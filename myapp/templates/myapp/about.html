Our Story

Founded in 2020, Oxen Fitness has grown from a small local gym to a premier fitness destination.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to provide a welcoming, inclusive environment where people of all fitness levels could achieve their goals. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of members who trust us with their fitness journey.

We believe in continuous improvement and staying at the forefront of fitness trends and technology. Our facility is regularly updated with the latest equipment and our trainers undergo ongoing professional development.
